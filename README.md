# Bharatiya Krishi Foundation

## Mission
Providing direct benefits of government schemes to farmers and bridging transparency between government and farmers.

## Vision
An India where every farmer gets the full benefit of government schemes, equipment, and offers without any middlemen.

## NGO Structure Recommendation

### 🏢 Section 8 Company (Recommended)
**Benefits:**
- Government recognition and credibility
- Better transparency and governance
- Easy access to CSR funds and government grants
- High trust factor with both government and farmers
- Ability to operate across multiple states

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 2 members
- Minimum 2 directors
- ₹1,00,000 authorized share capital
- Not for profit, for social purpose

**Process:**
1. **Name Approval**: From Registrar of Companies
2. **Document Preparation**: 
   - Memorandum of Association
   - Articles of Association
   - Board Resolution
3. **Registration**: Application with RoC
4. **License**: Section 8 license
5. **PAN and TAN**: Tax registration
6. **Bank Account**: For NGO

### 🏛️ Trust (Alternative)
**Benefits:**
- Simple compliance
- Less formalities
- Quick establishment

**Limitations:**
- Limited scalability
- Less government recognition
- Limited funding options
