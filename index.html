Our Mission

Providing direct benefits of government schemes to farmers

Why Bharatiya Krishi Foundation?

In today's time, farmers don't get the full benefit of government schemes. Due to black market and middlemen, farmers don't get their rights. Our goal is:

  • Transparent information of all government schemes
  • Direct connection of farmers with government
  • Real-time tracking of equipment
  • Stop black market practices
  • Voice of farmers to reach government
