About Bharatiya Krishi Foundation

Empowering farmers through education, technology, and sustainable practices since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Bharatiya Krishi Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to transform Indian agriculture and empower the backbone of our nation - our farmers.

What started as a small initiative in rural Maharashtra has grown into a nationwide movement, touching the lives of over 50,000 farmers across 25+ districts. Our journey began when our founders, Dr. Rajesh Kumar and Dr. Priya Sharma, witnessed the struggles of farmers in their village and decided to take action.

Today, we are proud to be one of India's leading agricultural NGOs, working tirelessly to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern agricultural technology.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with knowledge, technology, and resources to improve agricultural productivity, sustainability, and livelihoods through comprehensive education, modern farming techniques, and community development.

Our Vision

A prosperous India where every farmer has access to modern agricultural practices, fair markets, sustainable farming methods, and the dignity they deserve as the backbone of our nation's food security.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly farming practices that protect our environment for future generations.

Innovation

We embrace modern agricultural technology and innovative solutions to solve farming challenges.

Community

We build strong farmer communities that support and learn from each other.

Education

We believe in continuous learning and knowledge sharing to improve farming practices.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of transparency and ethical practices in all our work.

Empathy

We understand and respect the challenges farmers face and work with compassion.
